What is the SSB Interview?

The Services Selection Board (SSB) is a rigorous 5-day evaluation process that assesses candidates for their suitability to become officers in the Indian Armed Forces. It's not just a test of knowledge, but a deep analysis of personality, leadership potential, and psychological fitness.
